Welcome to Richmond Chess Club

Welcome to the Richmond Chess Club, where chess enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels come together to enjoy ,the game of kings. Our club is dedicated to promoting and fostering a love of chess in our community, and we welcome all who share this passion to join us. Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned player, our club provides a friendly and welcoming environment for you to improve your skills, meet new people, and engage in friendly competition. We offer weekly gatherings where members can come together to play chess, socialize, and learn from one another. Our club is committed to providing a safe and inclusive space for all members. We believe that chess is a game that can bring people together, and we strive to create a community where everyone feels welcome and respected.

Join us for our weekly chess gatherings!

Looking for a fun and engaging way to spend your weeknights? Come join the Richmond Chess Club for our weekly gatherings! Our club provides a welcoming and inclusive space for players of all skill levels to come together, play chess, and meet new friends. We look forward to seeing you there!
